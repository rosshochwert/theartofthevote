    Big Four
    Put your hands together for our best pick up opportunities.


    Arizona: Mark Kelly
    Kelly is a retired US Navy combat pilot, engineer and NASA astronaut — no biggie. He cares about health care, economy, gun safety and fixing the broken immigration system.

    Opponent: Martha McSally (Was appointed to fill the seat of the late Senator John McCain, so she hasn’t actually won an election)

    Colorado: John Hickenlooper
    Colorado has been reliably blue since 2014, so we have a good chance here. Hickenlooper is a former governor and ran for president in 2020. He cares about small businesses, gun safety and the environment. Fun fact — he opened the first brewpub in Colorado.

    Opponent: Senator Cory Gardner (Reliably votes with Trump and the Republicans)

    Maine: Sara Gideon
    Despite Maine going for Hillary in 2016 and the Democrats flipping the congressional district in 2018, it’s still dicey. Gideon is currently Maine Speaker of the House and cares about healthcare, the economy and education.

    Opponent: Senator Susan Collins (AKA the world’s most unpopular Senator)

    DONATE NOW


    North Carolina: Cal Cunningham
    North Carolina is considered another swing state in the general election and races are often won by a small margin. Cunningham is a former North Carolina state Senator, an Afghanistan and Iraq war veteran, and helped form an environmental waste reduction company.
