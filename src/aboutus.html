    The Mission
    We’re a group of Chicago creatives (and one programmer) who are passionate about art and politics. The website and stickers are a way to merge our two loves and hopefully make a tangible difference in the 2020 election. If we encourage just one person to vote, we’ll consider this endeavour a success
